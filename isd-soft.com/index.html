Extended Teams

Team extension, also known as team augmentation, allows you to extend your software development team with ISD experts (developer, tester, PM, architect etc.). You can acquire the necessary skills when needed and have full control over the development process.

Dedicated Teams

A dedicated team is formed only by ISD staff (developers, QA, PM, DevOps etc.) assigned exclusively to implement a particular software development project for your company. Dedicated teams are suited best for mid-large projects and mid-large term collaborations.

Fixed Price

Fixed Price model implies the implementation of a customer business idea only by the ISD experts, thus taking full responsibility for the project delivery. This service is usually offered within a predetermined timeframe and has fixed technical requirements.

Why software outsource with ISD?

Full transparency

Work with us transparently as if we sit in your office. Constant communication is provided via Teams/Slack, email and other means of communication. Our developers also make regular site visits to establish and maintain a smooth communication setup.

Unique expertise

We implement projects in various industries, but we excel in combining high-level programming languages as Java, .NET, JavaScript and specific technologies for programming hardware, embedded, PLC, IoT.

Best value for money

We offer an advantageous combination of cost and quality by building a dedicated development team, extending your project development team, or implementing a fixed price project. We provide fair rates to our customer but also competitive wages to our developers.

Strong partnership

We believe in long term partnerships, and we aim to establish a relationship based on trust and full commitment. We have developed internal programs to increase the technical capabilities of our developers, but also their capacity to establish a strong communication with our customers in order to better understand their needs and expectations.

Engineers at core

Our top priority is to make things working efficiently. Everyone you work with is professional, organized, and highly qualified for the work you need. Our talents conduct courses at the Technical University of Moldova and also, they mentor various educational programs for IT specialists. Our developers are certified by Oracle, Cisco, Microsoft & other providers.

Inther Software Development -
part of Inther Group

ISD is a spin-off of Inther Group, an international system integrator based in the Netherlands. We are located in Moldova and we started our journey as a part of Inthers development team before becoming a company in our own right.

Today, we continue to be a partner in strategic development and maintenance for Inther, and our collaboration has evolved into a 2-way relationship. Thus, Inther is also providing the necessary support for us and our clients, ensuring a solid back-up for our projects.

F.A.Q.

Do you have additional questions?

How soon can I have a dedicated team of 4 engineers/developers?

The process of onboarding and hiring is different from project to project, but on average we start our projects in about 2-3 months.

What qualifications ISD employees hold?

All ISD people hold either graduate or post-graduate degrees and all of them are fluent in English. Our talents conduct courses at the Technical University of Moldova and also, they mentor various educational programs for IT specialists. Our developers are certified by Oracle, Cisco, Microsoft & other providers.

Can I interview, or speak with the developers before hiring?

Definitely! We encourage our customers to have virtual meetings with the candidates and visit the ISD office to meet the entire team.

How can I make sure that the confidentiality is respected?

All our employees and partners signed a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) and we also sign an NDA with our customers. Thus, all employees involved in a project, are bound to discretion and confidentiality.

How does ISD ensure the delivery of professional services?

We believe in long term partnerships, and we aim to establish a relationship based on trust and full commitment. We have developed internal programs to increase the technical capabilities of our developers, but also their capacity to establish a strong communication with our customers in order to understand better their needs and expectations.

We are committed to creating a consistent customer experience alongside fostering an empowering environment for each team member.
