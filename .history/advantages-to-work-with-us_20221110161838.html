part of Inther Group

Advantages Outsourcing to Moldova

Cultural compatibility

When you decide to outsource, cultural differences should not be ignored. Through collaborations with companies from all across the world, software development teams from Moldova acquired the necessary intercultural skills to build a good communication with their clients.

A rich talent pool

Moldova has increased its ICT exports 10 times in the last 15 years and is currently ranked 59th out of 175 countries in the ICT Development Index. Yearly, 2000 ICT students graduate Moldova’s universities, most of them being educated by the Technical University of Moldova (TUM).

Cost reduction

Outsourcing services considerably reduce the cost of software development projects as companies avoid the expenses related to hiring & payroll processes, including recruitment, onboarding, insurance, taxes etc. Amongst the Eastern European destinations, Moldova is one of the most competitive countries.

Quality services

Moldova has a talent pool providing a large variety of quality services. From web development to automation, our developers and engineers can support your business to upscale, reduce the time-to-market, and be a step ahead of your competition.

Technical infrastructure

Moldova has one of the fastest wired Internet connections in the world, as well as one of the cheapest in terms of price per Mbit. Users can experience good quality services all over the country due to the developed infrastructure.

Language proximity

A large part of Moldova’s population is multilingual. The country’s official language is Romanian, but nearly all IT professionals speak English fluently. At the same time, Russian is the second most widely spoken language in the country.
